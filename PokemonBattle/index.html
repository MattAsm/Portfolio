What it this? You and a CPU are given a random pokemon to duke it out. But this isn't like a regular pokemon battle, type advantages don't exist. You have 4 moves: Attack, Defend, Bide, and Heal

Attack: A standard attack that takes the greater of the pokemons base Attack or Special Attack to deal damage.

Defend: A blocking move that uses the greater of the pokemons base Defense or Special Defense to protect itself. If the enemies attack is greater then your defense, you take the difference.

Heal: Heal 30 health

Bide: A move that increases your Attack, Defend and Heal by a multiplier. Every use increases the multiplier by x0.2 capping at x2.

FAQ: Base stats are according to the PokeAPI.co API and are assumed to be true to the games.
